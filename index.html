Biography

Born in 1994 in a small town of Red River delta, North Vietnam. His passion for photography driven him to explore and capture the beautiful sights of nature. He took a big step into the world of landscape photography after graduated from Academy of Finance in 2016. Over the years, Linh has developed a sizeable portfolio of images that celebrate the beauty of Vietnam landscape.

His work has been licensed and featured such as National Geographic, Great Big Story, Nikon, Vietnam Televison, Vietnam Tourism Boards, Vietnam Pictorial. He has several awards in photography: National Geographic My Best Photo 2017, National Geographic Traveler photo contest 2019, EPSON Pano Award 2020, Best Newcomer & People Choice Award at Royal Museum Greenwich Astro Photographer Of The Year photo contest 2020.
